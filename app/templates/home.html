What is Ask Napier?

Ask Napier is a message board/forum designed to allow students of the university to post messages to their course modules where other members will be able to reply.

This platform is completely anonymous and there is no way to identify the poster. This way it allows students to be completely open and will make it easier to ask questions. No need to worry about asking a silly question here.

To get started, simply register/sign-in to view a list of your modules message boards.
